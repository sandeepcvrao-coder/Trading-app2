🚀 WHAT CAUSES +50/80 POINT MOVES
📊
Quarterly Results
Company announces profits after 3 PM previous day. Stock gaps up 5-15% next morning. Check NSE website for results calendar.
📰
Big News / Contract
Defence order won, big export deal, government project announcement. PSU stocks like HINDCOPPER, BEL move huge on these.
🌍
Global Market Rally
US markets close strong overnight → Indian IT & metal stocks gap up big. Check Dow Jones close before sleeping every night!
💰
FII/DII Bulk Buying
Foreign investors buying huge quantities shows in pre-market. Stocks in their buy list gap up 2-5%. Check NSE bulk deals page.
⚡
Upper Circuit Stocks
Small/mid cap stocks hit upper circuit at 9:15. These move +10-20% in single candle. Very high risk — only for experienced traders.
🏦
Sector Rally
Government announces policy for a sector → entire sector moves together. All banking stocks up together, all metal stocks up together.
